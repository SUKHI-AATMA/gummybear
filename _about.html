About Us.

Aye! Gidday! We strive to grow together with our clients. At Gummy Bear, we have a simple goal: to create WOW service for you, be the most-loved Digital Creative Agency in the South Island and to do so at your Service, Aye! So, we opened a Dictionary at a few random pages and started choosing various whacky names for our company. The names didn't have to make much sense really. E.g., Paper Boat, Paper Planes, Mason Jar, Lonely Kiwi etc. Finally, since the creative juices of us MBAs didn't seem to flow, we looked towards the best, original, unbiased and purest source for consultation and advise like every professional would. So, we spoke with Montessori kids as to what name would they consider for their company that is relatable, has a strong story to tell and is an emotional-cultural expression! Guess what, ‘Gummy Bear’ is a real company now. Then we thought of a strapline which basically tells our clients nothing about what we do, but in short: ‘We make you presentable on the Internet.’ We also thought of ‘We make Dope-Shit!’ but dropped the idea for obvious reasons. We have created a whacky ‘Team Bear’ page and included useless facts about our people, some paid-to-shoot-studio edited photos, basically anything that will make you our clients not want to actually meet our team. Smart, aye? Now we will talk incessantly about ‘Our brand’, ‘Our work’, ‘Storytelling’ and blah. We work from a slightly run-down, open-plan office in an up-and-coming part of town and have purchased some really colourful, abstract and uncomfortable furniture. We also have hung a bike on the wall (against exposed brick, yes). We have breakout room and a piece of novelty sporting equipment such as a pinball machine. And a mini fridge too. We have MacBooks with stickers on them from some free events. We do not have artists or developers but ‘Design Rockstar’ and ‘Code Ninjas’ etc. We have more Directors than the actual working staff. All our team members wear thick, black-rimmed designer glasses, brown-shoes with blue-jeans. We always have loads of fun ideas and have scattered toys and Legos around at the office. We have an actual Cat as a team member, just to look cool. We follow every single design, social and marketing trend just like every other creative-agency in the world and we love to brag about industry thought leadership on our social media to our total 37 followers. It doesn’t matter if it’s good, It only matters if it ROCKS. People don't buy products or services — they buy results, benefits and solutions. our website is a Lie. But we are real people, solving real digital problems of our clients. Please talk to us!
